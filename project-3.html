Project Overview

The platform is designed to replace outdated training models and static business presentations
with interactive, web-based solutions. It brings educational content delivery and corporate
branding under one unified digital roof.
The LMS side of Techonsy offers structured learning paths, interactive modules, assessments,
and progress tracking — catering to students, job seekers, and upskilling professionals.
On the corporate side, it highlights company services like Full Stack Development, UI/UX Design,
Cloud Solutions, and QA Testing through modern, responsive design.
